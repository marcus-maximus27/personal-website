## The idea

I keep coming back to one thing: the music you love says something about **who you are**, but the tools we use to listen keep it locked away in private playlists. I started building [mezzo](/#projects) to turn that taste into something you can share and shape.

Writing posts is simple — every post is a Markdown file in the `_posts` folder. You write in plain text, push to GitHub, and the site rebuilds itself.

## A few formatting examples

You get headings, **bold**, *italics*, and [links](https://marcusmaximus.ca) for free.

- Bullet lists work
- So do numbered lists
- And nested ideas

> Block quotes look like this — handy for pulling out a key line.

`inline code` and code blocks are styled too:

```
const taste = profile.songs.rank();
```

That's it. Add a new file, push, and you've published.
